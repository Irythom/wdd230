Heading Font

I have chosen Montserrat due to it being a neat, clean, and very legible font to read. I enjoy things that are nice and simplistic, and modern, without too many frills attached. This gives off a very professional appearance.

Paragraph Font

I have chosen Montserrat for the same reasons as previously stated. I thought long and hard about what font would go well with Montserrat as a paragraph font, and the more options I looked at the less visually appealing they were. I then took a look at a couple of websites. Specifically Apple, and mejuri. Both websites are very successful, aesthetically pleasing, websites that I have heard compliments on from friends and family. In fact, I specifcially asked friends and family about their favorite websites to navigate and these were the top two. I believe that replicating this single font will allow me to similarly provide a pleasing user experience.
