Target Audience

The two main group of members that this chamber of commerce is supposed to reach are somewhat talked about on the main page: local businesses and individuals of Eagle Mountain.

Personas

The personas listed below are ficticous examples of people who may be interested in viewing Eagle Mountain's chamber of commerce website.

Joe Dearden; Owner of local shop named Quench it: At 43 years old, Joe Dearden has just opened a local shop where he sells a variety of custom ordered drinks. He is a hardworker, who singlehandedly started the shop all on his own. However, due to very few connections to the community, as of yet no one has shown interest in his new shop. He is looking for somewhere to connect him with others around him, and is looking to receive some support and advice in his new endeavors.

Alan Benedict; Local resident: Alan is just your average neighbor. At 45 years old, he has a family of 5, a stable job, and is quite content with where he lives. A generous guy at heart, he is one to buy girlscout cookies not for the cookies themselves, but to help those girlscouts out in the way that he can. Alan absolutely loves to socialize, and is all for making new friends. He has been looking for community events to attend, so that he can continue to meet more people.

Scenarios

  • How do I connect with other local businesses, and potentially work together with them?
  • Where can I find local events?
  • How can I help develop/change things in my community?
  • How do I get my name or my company's name out there?
  • What service opportunities can my community provide?
