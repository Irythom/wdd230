Discover Eagle Mountain

Welcome to Eagle Mountain!

Eagle Mountain's Cedar Valley Highschool opened in 2019. Home of the Aviators.

Cory B Wride Memorial Park was named after a police officer killed in the line of duty. At this park you will find a giant playground, 4 baseball diamonds, a splash pad, zip-lines, swings, basketball courts, tennis courts, pickleball courts, pavilions, along with a festival field.

The Eagle Mountain Petryoglyphs can be found all over. The most popular of these are named the Three Warriors. As recently as the mid-1800s, the Ute Indians inhabited Utah Valley and fished Utah lake extensively, with the lake being a substantial food source. The area around Utah lake is home to many petroglyphs.

The Eagle Mountain Ranch Bike Park offers the best stunt biking within Utah county. From a skills park to pump tracks, jump lines, and multiple techincal trails, there is plenty to do for both the inexperienced, and veteran riders

The Eagle Mountain Skate Park is filled with ramps, rails, a halfpipe, a bowl, and a bunch of benches, it's the best place in Eagle Mountain to sharpen your skating skills.

The Silver Lake Amphitheater is a great place to take in a concert, movie nights in the park, and a variety of other events. There is plenty of seating on the grass surrounding the amphitheater and standing room in front of the stage.

The Snoasis Snowcone Shack is a popular location for locals to flock to during those hot summer days. Providing a plethora of commonly known flavors, along with some Snoasis originals, there are plenty of flavors to try!

The Ranches Golf Club is a spectacular 18-hole championship facility nestled in the hillside valley between Lehi and Eagle Mountain. Featuring a Western theme, The Ranches Golf Club offers golfers a relaxing retreat with acres of elbow room to enjoy.
