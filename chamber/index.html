Site Purpose

The purpose of this organization is for the local businesses and individuals of Eagle Mountain to band together, businesses creating opportunities to give back to the community, and the community creating opportunities to give back to the businesses. As Eagle Mountain bands together to accomplish one goal, we can work towards developing our city in the way that we as the people of Eagle Mountain would like. We can make the changes we want and need, and can grow in ways that wouldn't be possible without the support we can receive from eachother!
