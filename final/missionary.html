Extended Stay

Temple Inn & Suites provides long term accommodations designed to be rented out to full time temple missionaries. We provide top of the line extended stay accommodations, that are perfect for those looking for apartment-style amenities. Enjoy free WiFi, in-room kitchens, a queen sized mattress, separate shower and bath, and on-site laundry facilities at a budget-friendly hotel designed for longer stays. To schedule an online tour, visit our contact us page, and express interest. We will send an email to set up details for a zoom call where we walk through all room accommodations and questions.
