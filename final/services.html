Specialized Services

  • Free access to changing rooms and bathroom facilities for temple patrons who have traveled far and need to change to and from church clothes
  • A playroom and low-cost, short-term babysitting services for the children of patrons
  • Temple history themed reception and sitting area
  • Family search center that supports family search and ordinance work
  • Wedding reception hall that can be easily customized with a simple catering menu and kitchen access for food preparation and serving
  • Long-term stay accommodations in kitchenette suites for full-time temple missionaries ONLY
  • For more information on our wedding reception venue, or long-term housing, please refer to: Receptionsand Full-time Accommodations

Free Amenities

  • All following amenities are provided alongside the purchase of a room at Temple Inn & Suites:
  • Daily continental breakfast, made fresh. Gluten Free options available.
  • Exercise/workout room. TV's with provided workout routines available. Equipment ranging from treadmills and ellipticals, to heavy lifting equipment.
  • A pool, hot tub, and sauna at each of our locations.
  • WiFi high-speed internet access and smart TVs.
  • Provided parking spaces, designated specifically for the individual.
  • Temple shuttle that runs between the Inn and the nearby Temple every hour.
  • Family history consultation and family ordinance card service.
