About the Wedding Hall

Temple Inn & Suites provides a classic, and grand wedding hall to rent out. Designed to be palatial, and elegant, our goal is to create a calm environment to mimic even just a fraction of the simultaneous awe and serenity that a temple brings. We aim to provide you with the perfect venue to host your dream reception or next corporate event.

What's included?

  • Included with rental:
  • Venue space
  • Table and chairs
  • Chandelier
  • Audio equipment and projector
  • Variety of colored linens for room
  • Food prep area
  • Optional rentals (extra charge):
  • Table runners
  • Variety of signs
  • Dinnerware sets

Please note that our renters will need to provide decorations, including flower arrangements. We provide outsourced, local catering. Use our contact us page to ask about flourists and vendors/caterers we reccommend for your specific location, as it varies. All setup of items included with rental will be provided by the house.
